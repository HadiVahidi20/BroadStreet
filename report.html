Sitemap Content Notes (1-line per page)

  • Home: headline overview, next match, recent results, and main calls to action.
  • Fixtures & Results: full schedule with filters and recent scores.
  • League Table: current standings and points for the competition.
  • Matchday: directions, parking, timings, and on-site facilities.
  • News: club updates, announcements, and press-style posts.
  • Fan Zone: social feeds, photos, videos, and supporter content.
  • Sponsors / Business: sponsorship options, partner logos, and inquiry form.
  • Clubhouse / Venue Hire: venue spaces, capacities, and booking details.
  • Contact: contact methods, key emails, and location map.
  • Teams: all squads with coaches, training, and roster info.
  • About / History: club story, milestones, and values.
  • Membership / Join: membership types, fees, and how to sign up.
  • Gallery / Media: curated photos and videos from matches and events.
  • Community / Outreach: community programs, schools, and charity work.
  • Safeguarding / Policies: safeguarding contacts and key policies.
  • Shop / Kit: official merchandise and kit ordering.
  • Volunteering: roles needed and how to get involved.

Page Goals and Primary CTAs

  • Home: highlight next match, latest results, and direct links to tickets, fixtures, and sponsor inquiries.
  • Fixtures & Results: quick access to the next match, recent scores, and league position.
  • Matchday: reduce visitor friction with directions, parking, timetable, and facilities.
  • Fan Zone: keep supporters engaged through photos, social links, and community content.
  • Sponsors / Business: convert interest into leads via packages, benefits, and contact form.
  • Clubhouse / Venue Hire: showcase spaces and capture booking inquiries.
  • Contact: clear pathways for general inquiries, rugby, sponsorship, and events.

Key Conversion Pages

  • Fixtures & Results: next match, recent results, table or standings.
  • Matchday: directions, parking, facilities, food and drink, schedule.
  • Sponsors / Business: sponsorship packages, benefits, contact form.
  • Clubhouse / Venue Hire: spaces, capacity, amenities, gallery, inquiry form.

Reference Site Findings: RC Toulon (What Helps Our Project)

Information Architecture Patterns

  • Top bar elevates revenue and experience paths: ticketing, shop, business, and events/hospitality.
  • Main navigation groups content into News, Pro Team (calendar, results, standings, squad), Teams (youth pathway), Fan Zone, Club history/stadium, and CSR/engagement.
  • Fan Zone acts as a hub for ticketing, shop, photos, social links, matchday info, and supporter clubs.

Match + Ticketing Experience

  • Homepage highlights the next home match with a clear “Reserve” call to action plus upcoming fixtures.
  • League standings and recent results are visible on the main site to keep supporters updated.
  • Ticketing runs on a dedicated sub-site with its own navigation: calendar, subscriptions, offers, VIP, practical info, and resale.
  • Upcoming matches are displayed as cards with date/time and a strong “Reserve” button.
  • Ticketing page emphasizes trust signals (official ticketing, secure payment, digital tickets) and displays sponsor tiers.

Fan Zone + Matchday Engagement

  • Fan Zone posts promote pre-match activities (food trucks, music, kids’ activities, giveaways).
  • Matchday information includes practical guidance and safety rules (access, prohibited items, timing).
  • Supporter services like carpooling are promoted via Fan Zone and the club app.

Commercial & Hospitality

  • Separate “Business” and “Events/Seminars” paths reinforce sponsorship and venue hire as distinct revenue streams.
  • Cross-links between the main site, ticketing, and shop keep users inside the club ecosystem.

Ideas to Borrow for Broadstreet

  • Give “Next Match” top priority on Home with a bold CTA and secondary fixtures list.
  • Keep standings visible (widget or mini-table) on Home and Fixtures.
  • Create a Fan Zone page that bundles matchday info, media, social, and supporter clubs.
  • Use a dedicated Sponsors/Business page and a separate Clubhouse/Venue Hire page, both with short inquiry forms.
  • Add trust signals and sponsor tiers near ticket/RSVP actions.
