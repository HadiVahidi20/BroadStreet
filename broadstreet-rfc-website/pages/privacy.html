Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

Broadstreet RFC is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and contact information
  • Membership details
  • Payment information
  • Medical information (for players)
  • Photos and videos at club events

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Manage your membership
  • Communicate club news and events
  • Process payments
  • Comply with RFU requirements
  • Ensure player welfare and safety

Data Protection

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information and only retain it for as long as necessary.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at info@broadstreet-rugby.co.uk for any data requests.

Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@broadstreet-rugby.co.uk.
