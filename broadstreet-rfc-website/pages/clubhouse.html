Your perfect venue for events, celebrations, and corporate functions in Coventry.

Make an Enquiry

A Unique Venue for Every Occasion

The Clubhouse at Broadstreet RFC offers a versatile, welcoming space for your next event. Whether you're planning a corporate meeting, birthday celebration, wedding reception, or community gathering, our facilities can accommodate your needs.

Set within the grounds of our historic rugby club, The Clubhouse combines modern amenities with traditional character. With views over the pitches, ample parking, and flexible spaces, we provide a memorable backdrop for events of all sizes.

  • Capacity up to 150 guests
  • Licensed bar and catering available
  • Free on-site parking
  • Accessible facilities
  • AV equipment available
  • Outdoor space for larger events
