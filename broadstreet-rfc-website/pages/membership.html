Youth & Junior Rugby

We welcome young players from age 5 upwards. Our qualified coaches provide age-appropriate training in a safe, fun environment.

Age Groups

  • Minis (U6-U12): Introduction to rugby through games and skills
  • Juniors (U13-U16): Development of technical skills and team play
  • Colts (U17-U18): Transition to senior rugby

Training Times

Minis: Sunday mornings 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM
Juniors: Thursday evenings 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience to join?

No! We welcome players of all abilities, from complete beginners to experienced players. Our coaches will help you develop your skills at your own pace.

What do I need to bring to training?

Just bring suitable sports clothing, boots (moulded studs recommended), and a gum shield. We can advise on equipment when you join.

Can I try before I commit?

Absolutely! We offer trial sessions so you can experience training and meet the team before committing to membership.

Is there women's rugby?

Yes! We have a thriving women's section with teams at various levels. Contact us for more information about women's rugby at Broadstreet.
