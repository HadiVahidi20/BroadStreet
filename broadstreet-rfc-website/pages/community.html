Rugby in the Community

At Broadstreet RFC, we believe rugby is for everyone. Our community programmes bring the benefits of sport to people of all ages and backgrounds in Coventry.

Schools Programme

We work with local primary and secondary schools across Coventry and Warwickshire to introduce young people to rugby union.

What We Offer

  • Free taster sessions and coaching at schools
  • Tag rugby festivals for primary schools
  • After-school rugby clubs
  • Pathway to junior teams at the club
  • DBS-checked, qualified coaches

Partner Schools

We currently work with over 15 local schools including Binley Woods Primary, Stretton Primary, and Ernesford Grange Secondary.

Charity Support

Supporting local and national charities through fundraising events and awareness campaigns.

Charity Partners

Each season we partner with selected charities to raise funds and awareness:

  • Wooden Spoon - children's charity of rugby
  • MIND - mental health awareness
  • Local foodbanks
  • Restart Rugby - injured players fund

Fundraising Events

Throughout the year we host events to support our charity partners:

  • Charity matches and tournaments
  • Sponsored runs and cycles
  • Quiz nights and social events
  • Christmas toy collections

Rugby for All

We're committed to making rugby accessible and welcoming to everyone, regardless of age, ability, or background.

Walking Rugby

A slower-paced version of the game for those who want to stay active. Perfect for returning to sport or staying fit as we age.

When: Thursdays 7pm

Mixed Ability Rugby

Inclusive rugby for players of all abilities, bringing together disabled and non-disabled players in the same team.
