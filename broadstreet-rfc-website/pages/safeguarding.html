Safeguarding

Broadstreet RFC is committed to providing a safe environment for all members, especially children and vulnerable adults.

Club Safeguarding Officer

For any safeguarding concerns, please contact our Club Safeguarding Officer at safeguarding@broadstreet-rugby.co.uk

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Providing a safe environment for children and young people
  • Implementing the RFU Safeguarding Policy
  • Ensuring all coaches and volunteers undergo DBS checks
  • Providing safeguarding training for all relevant personnel
  • Responding appropriately to any safeguarding concerns

Reporting Concerns

If you have any concerns about the welfare of a child or vulnerable adult at the club, please:

  1. Contact the Club Safeguarding Officer immediately
  2. If the concern is urgent and the CSO is unavailable, contact the RFU Safeguarding Team on 0800 021 4180
  3. In an emergency, contact the police on 999

RFU Resources

For more information about safeguarding in rugby, visit the RFU Safeguarding page.
