First Team

1st XV Squad

Tradition. Resilience. Ambition.

The Broadstreet RFC 1st XV is the flagship side of the club and a team shaped by experience at the higher levels of English rugby. With a proud history of competing across National 2, Regional 1, and now Regional 2 Midlands East, the 1st XV is driven by one clear goal - to climb back up the leagues and re-establish Broadstreet at the level it belongs.
